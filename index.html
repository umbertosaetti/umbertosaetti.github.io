Dr. Umberto Saetti
Postdoctoral Fellow

About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow with the School of Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. My research interests are in the areas of rotorcraft flight dynamics, controls, handling qualities, and system identification.
I hold a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering, a M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering, and a M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Pennsylvania State University. I received my B.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, Italy.
I am currently seeking a tenure-track faculty position at the assistant professor level in the area of flight dynamics and controls.
